# nCoV_News_App
* nCoV News App help people Tracking COVID-19 Cases in Vietnam and the world, user can read new News about Covid in VnExpress 
* I have developed this app all by myself and I would be happy to share the APK of the app for testing purposes

### Prequisites
-   shimmer
-   flutter_bloc
-   google_fonts
-   flare_flutter
-   flutter_webview_plugin
-   carousel_slider
-   modal_bottom_sheet
-   cached_network_image
-   RadpidAPI Covid Tracking
-   RSS of VnExpress 

### Attractions
-   Carousel News
-   Carousel Covid Case , Death, Recovered in the World
-   Slider Animation
-   Flare animation
-   Tracking Covid Data 
-   Read News health RSS VnExpress
